I am an entrepreneur who combines a technical background with a wide industry vision and who is driven by the achievement of commercial targets.

Before full-time software engineering, in 2000 I built one of the most popular websites world-wide about biketrial (important sponsorships, world-wide travel), organized sports international competitions and events for marketing actions, being trusted by brands like Braun or Powerade.

As a software engineer my professional background is diverse, ranging from freelancing to support my studies, successfully leading accreditation efforts for an acquired startup to engineering solutions for big names in the communications industry like AT&T or Fox.

My area of expertise is Messaging as a Platform, developed while working for the past years developing state of the art messaging platforms for telecoms. These concepts are nowadays being introduced by social media companies for the Internet sector to leverage them in creating new and more effective communication experiences.

My particular vision is to help businesses leverage the immediacy chat provides for e-commerce use cases. The wide adoption, the personal context and the simpler experience chat enables will help online stores closing more deals in the next years.

My mission is to be at the forefront in the creation of those new tools and to do it based on my personal and professional principles: learn from mistakes, keep a crystal clear vision, move forward checkpoint after checkpoint and just focus on building useful tools.
