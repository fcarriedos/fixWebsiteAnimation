A high-quality solution for those who want a beautiful startup website quickly.

We have created this demo version in order to show you the structure of Startup Framework. It has some of the
components from the full version, 2 great samples (Bootstrap themes) and documentation. You can also find 2
images of a Macbook and an iPad, which you can use in your project. We hope you will like your first introduction
to Startup Framework.

The most important part of the Startup Framework are the samples or Bootstrap themes and templates. The samples consist of a set of few pages you can use "as is" or add new blocks from UI Kit. By choosing one of the 2 configurations of the future startup, the process of creation is simple and easy. You can decide whether to create your site using UI Kit components or samples.
